I have an Oakley insulated jacket for sale, yellow, size large loose fit. I used this jacket a couple seasons and it is in excellent condition. The material is very durable and has great waterproofing and breathability. The insulation is also very warm without being as bulky as a down jacket. All the pockets you need and it also has vents that go diagonal across the chest (can see in the pics) instead of the arm pits making them much more functional. Another one of my favorite features is the flap that covers the zipper is secured via magnets instead of more common snaps making it very easy to undo and close with gloves on. They are also strong enough to keep the jacket closed alone so you can keep the zipper low if you need to get in your jacket a lot. Its a great jacket, the only reason I am selling is because I have six ski jackets and do not use this one anymore.
